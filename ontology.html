L4 · AEO capsule layer

Sixty FAQ-rich capsules, targeted at LLM citation

Why 135 pages, not 500+

For consumer apps (like SureSpace), programmatic can stretch to 1,000+ pages because the long-tail is deep. B2B SaaS RevOps has a much shallower long-tail — maybe 200-300 genuine commercial queries total. 135 targeted pages covers 80% of them, with room for 50+ more in Year 2. Going higher without demand signal produces thin content that hurts topical authority instead of building it.
